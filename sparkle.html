Rules and Regulations

  1. >> Participants should submit application form duly completed in all respects.

  2. >> Incomplete applications will not be considered to participate in the event.

  3. >> Participants should be aware of the details of their category.

  4. >> Only solo singing is allowed for Singing competition.

  5. >> Registration can also be done on the spot of the audition before 10.am.

  6. >> Dance participants should bring CD on their own, containing only a song to which they/he/she going to perform (Any style must not exceed 3 minutes).
